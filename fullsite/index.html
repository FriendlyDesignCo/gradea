Grade A Leadership

Amanda Fisher, CEO of Grade A Marketing

After moving from St. Louis, MO to attend The George Washington University in Washington, DC, Amanda worked at DC101 Radio, WB-Television (before it was the CW), and Fleishman Hillard, International public relations. She held high level directorial and management positions in business development and marketing before deciding to start her own cross-media marketing and consulting firm in Dec. 2010 called Grade A Marketing.

Amanda has been featured in Executive Travel Magazine and ABC’s Washington Business Report. She is also Co-Founder of HD Happy Hour, an organization born in June 2009 that hosts monthly networking events for business owners and executives in the DC Metro Area.

Grade A Values

We really like our clients and partners and believe in establishing long-term, quality relationships with a “give first” mentality.

We do a few things to make sure those relationships flourish:

  1. We seize opportunities that will benefit clients
  2. We challenge traditional sales and marketing assumptions when there’s a better way of doing something
  3. We take responsibility for our decisions and stand behind our projects
  4. We respect and appreciate our clients and partners and the opportunities we have to work together
